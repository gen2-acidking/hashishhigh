    • About Hashishigh Designs

    We make functional and tested designs, our philosophy is heavily focused on usability and pragmatism. It is our firm belief, that all designs achieve their peak aesthetic form by following the principles of robustness, minimalism and most importantly, functionality.

    What We Do

    We design really good bongs, grinders and all sorts of accesories and distribute the STL files and manufacturing guides for personal and commercial use for a competetive price. We are working on solutions for selling the files directly on our platform, but for now the designs are available at Patreon and Cults3D packaged for different use cases. Cults3D contains desing packs, personal and commercial for individual product lines or collection. Whereas the Patreon cotains the full catalog with commercial and personal tiers available.

    We started with the RGB (Really Good Bong) collection: 48 water pipe designs across 4 base configurations. Each one is print-ready, and built around the 18.8mm standard joint that works with everything. And honeslty? they came out so good that we're not stopping. Stay tuned for grinders, containers, extendo systems, and much more.

    We also work with headshops and manufactures to provide branded versions of our designs, for these types of inquiries please reach out via the 'Contact' page and we can discuss custom branding solutions, or introduce yourself to our branding concept located at the 'Branding' page.

    What You Get

    Each collection purchase includes:

    • High-quality STL files, organized and named properly
    • Detailed README with print settings (layer height, infill, supports, temps)
    • Material recommendations (PETG, ABS, what works and why)
    • Dimensional specs for every component
    • Assembly notes for multi-part designs
    • Licensing terms (personal vs commercial usage)

    No DRM, no online activation, no subscription locks. You buy it, you own the files, print as many as you want within your license terms. All licenses are perpetual and do not expire.

    Community & Support

    We're actively developing new designs (grinders, containers, extendo systems, round beakers). Patreon members get early access and input on development priorities.

    Custom design requests and commercial licensing inquiries are welcome. We can modify existing designs or create completely new pieces for your specific needs.
