    The Concept

    Hashishigh Designs provides really good collections of robust cannabis accesories. What is memorable about these products is how plain good they are. So why not make the other memorable thing, your brand?

    Your logos or artwork can be integrated directly into these functional designs with bespoke service to just simply get you what you want, we can completly structure the service around your needs.

    Global Branding Solutions

    We will work with you or your team to create branded versions of any collection you want, or even completly custom collections. We will also work with you on finding local manufacturing solutions in your area based on the local manufacturing experience we have in Finland.

    If you happen to be located in Finland or reasonably close with a reasonably dense item, we can manufacture and ship the product directly to you from our Finnish operations through our brand Hasishai. Unfortunately shipping low cost, hollow and large items internationally is not feasible for us at the moment.
