    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I upgrade from Personal to Commercial later?

    Yes. Contact us at sgs@sgs.sh with your original purchase proof and we'll provide an upgrade path. You'll pay the difference between license types ($84).

    Do I need separate licenses for each collection?

    Yes. Each collection requires its own license. If you want access to all designs with one payment, consider our Patreon tiers ($50/mo personal, $300/mo commercial).

    Can I sell prints on Etsy, eBay, or at maker fairs?

    Yes, with a Commercial License. You can sell physical prints anywhere, online or in-person. Just don't distribute the STL files.

    What if I modify the design significantly?

    With a Commercial License, you can modify and sell the physical products. However, you cannot create and sell derivative STL files, even if heavily modified.

    Can I use these designs for a print-on-demand service?

    With a Commercial License, yes - as long as the service provides physical products to customers, not downloadable files. You must own the license, not your customers.

    What happens if I violate the license?

    License termination, requirement to cease all use, and potential legal action for copyright infringement. We monitor major platforms for unauthorized file distribution.

    Can I share files with my print farm employees?

    Yes, with a Commercial License. You can use the files across your business operations and share with employees for manufacturing purposes only.
